About

What is this?

Astra Solaris is a set of software projects dedicated to astronomy and astronautics. All Astra Solaris projects are open source under MIT licence. The Institute of Astronomy and Meteorology of the University of Guadalajara is official partner and main scientific collaborator. You can contact us for participating in the development. Collaboration is NOT restricted to scientists and programmers.

Astra Solaris is growing every day

More projects and more functions are added frecuently to the astra solaris repository. Visit Astra Solaris in github You can also help this project to grow.

Be Part of Our Community

If you want to participate in any project do not hesitate to contact us. Scientist, engineers, programmers, designers, and any space enthusiast are wellcome in our community. You can join us and meet other people in the community who share the same interests with you.

Support Astra Solaris

Astra Solaris is a project powered by a very small team, but an ever growing community, and depends on people volunteering time, code, money, and energy to keep it going.
